    Laparoscopic Urology

    In urological operations using keyhole or minimally invasive or laparoscopic techniques. The benefits of laparoscopic surgery include less blood loss, less pain and faster recovery.Patient hospital stay would also be much shorter when compared to traditional open surgery. Swastik Hospital offer laparoscopic surgeries for benign and malignant renal tumours, laparoscopic varicocelectomy, laparoscopic nephrectomy, laparoscopic adrenalectomy, laparoscopic ureterolithotomy, to reconstructive surgeries such as laparoscopic pyeloplasty and laparoscopic hernia repair and complex ablative surgery, such as laparoscopic radical prostatectomy.

    Kidney Stone Treatment and Endourology

    Stones are mostly formed in the kidneys and may get stuck in the ureter, blocking the flow of urine and causing pain. Urinary stone disease is one of the most common urological problems treated at Swastik hospitals & Urological centre. The hospitals have complete range of endourological techniques, minimally invasive procedures, ultrasound and laser non-surgical treatment and the latest technological innovations to treat kidney stone problem. Treatments such as non-invasive Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL), Holmium Laser Lithotripsy (endoscopic laser surgery) or Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy (PCNL) are offered at our network hospitals.
